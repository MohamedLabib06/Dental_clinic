About the Doctor

Meet Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Lead Dentist & Founder

Dr. Sarah Mitchell is a board-certified dentist with over 15 years of experience in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. She graduated with honors from Harvard School of Dental Medicine and has completed advanced training in implantology and digital smile design.

Her philosophy centers on patient comfort and minimally invasive techniques. Dr. Mitchell believes every patient deserves personalized care in a stress-free environment.

  • Harvard School of Dental Medicine, DDS
  • Fellow, American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry
  • 2,000+ successful implant procedures
  • Featured in Dental Health Magazine
